ABOUT APA

APA Industries Co., Ltd. was established in 1964, at present a registered capital of 250 million Bath(USD 8 million). With over 50 years of Operation, we are experienced in the production and quality control of high caliber pharmaceutical and cosmetic primary packaging. APA Industries Co., Ltd. Is committed to delivering high-quality products to its customer. Its production facility is equipped with up-to-date, high-precision production machineries as well as quality control equipment, which are regularly calibrated and maintained to ensure quality,and safety of the products. APA Industries Co., Ltd. Follows stringent ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14644 standards, and continues to improve the manufacturing and quality control methods to ensure that all of its products meet the set standards and exceed the customer expectations.
